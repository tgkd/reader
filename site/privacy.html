Privacy Policy

Effective as of 2026-07-03

This policy describes what data the Yomi (読み) iOS application handles and where it goes. The short version: your books live on your device, there are no accounts, no ads, and no analytics — and text leaves your device only when you ask for a paid AI feature.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No user accounts, names, email addresses, or profiles.
  • No advertising identifiers or third-party ad SDKs.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking.
  • No location data.

Your Books

Books you import (EPUB, PDF, TXT) are stored locally on your device. Reading them — including furigana and dictionary lookups, which are computed on-device — sends nothing anywhere. Deleting a book removes it and its generated audio from your device.

Processing for Paid AI Features

Two features send parts of your content off the device, and only when you explicitly request them:

  • Speech generation: when you press Play on a chapter without generated audio, that chapter's text is sent through our proxy server (Cloudflare) to ElevenLabs to synthesize narration.
  • AI text recognition: when you confirm reading a scanned or image-only book, its page images are sent through our proxy server to Google (Gemini) to recover the text.

This content is processed to produce the result and returned to your device, where it is stored (generated audio is cached locally and can be cleared in Settings). We do not build a library of your content on our servers. The third-party providers process the data under their own terms: ElevenLabs, Google, Cloudflare.

Subscription Data

Subscriptions are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, which assigns an anonymous identifier to your installation and stores your purchase and entitlement state. That anonymous identifier accompanies paid requests to our proxy server so it can verify an active subscription; the verification result is cached server-side. We never see your payment details — Apple handles billing.

Data Retention

  • Books, generated audio, and preferences: on your device, until you delete them or the app.
  • Text and images sent for AI processing: processed transiently; not retained as content on our servers (providers' own retention policies apply, per their links above).
  • Subscription state: retained by RevenueCat/Apple per their policies; our server keeps only a short-lived verification cache keyed by the anonymous identifier.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

All network communication uses HTTPS. Paid requests are authorized against your anonymous subscription identifier server-side; API keys for AI providers never leave our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this page; the effective date above reflects the latest revision.
